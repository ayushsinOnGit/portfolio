Hello, this is Ayush

CreateWithAyush

Greetings, world! I’m Ayush—professional code wrangler, gym enthusiast, and accidental gadget disassembler since the age of five. Forget dusty stereotypes: this Compiler Engineer at AMD stays busy fine-tuning GPUs, cracking cryptic puzzles, and occasionally bench-pressing more than just lines of code.

I cut my teeth on toy robots, spent my days tinkering with compilers at Jadavpur University, and now I make GPU magic for a living. When I'm not making code dance, I'm usually off snapping pics on my travels or jotting down musings on my blog—where GPU wizards, weightlifters, and curious souls alike are always welcome.

So dive in, explore my eclectic corner of tech meets adventure, and get ready for a few chuckles along the way. After all, life’s too short not to have fun while spinning up blazing-fast code!

Whoami?

Ever since I could pick up a screwdriver, I’ve been dissecting everything from toy robots to my parents’ patience. While they endured a living room covered in springs and circuits, I discovered a passion for compilers, GPUs, and all things code.

That curiosity soared at Jadavpur University, where I dove into the fascinating worlds of formal verification, computer architecture, and puzzle-like software optimization. Fast-forward to now, and I'm a Compiler Engineer at AMD, making GPUs sing while helping code pirouette at lightning speed.

When I’m not flexing my brain cells, you’ll find me lifting weights, snapping travel shots, and scribbling quirky insights on my blog—stay tuned for my travel blog too! Swing by, say hi, and let’s build something awesome together.
